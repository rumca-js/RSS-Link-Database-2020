Born from modular, reborn with the Hydrasynth in Ableton.
Get the 4-song EP: https://fanlink.to/rmr_be_the_one

Patch notes: 

This is a really simple patch, really. Just using the system like a groovebox. 
Hermod is sequencing the Piston Honda, Plaits, and Pico Voice and O-Coast. I had some tuning issues on the Pico Voice I fixed in post for the released track. I use the mute track function on the Hermod to bring parts in and out. The sample is coming through the Radio Music module through the Harvestman Polivoks Filter. Drums are from the vpme QD and Pico Drum 2, sequenced via the KeyStep Pro. Vocals are processed through the Perform VE, and the Hydrasynth is doing pads.
